International Open Data Day 2015 - Hamburg Edition

Learn about Open Data and hack on projects! The German Open Knowledge community and Wikipedia are hosting a hackday for Open Data Day 2015.

This event is for designers, developers, journalists, activists, and everyone else who is interested in learning about Open Data or contributing to Open Data projects.

After a short introduction into the concept of Open Data we will present project ideas for people with various skill sets and levels of experience. Of course, you are more than welcome to bring your own Open Data projects and find collaborators!

  • 13:00 Introduction
  • 14:00 Kick off hackday
  • ~ 19:00 Wrap up und eventuelle Pizza.
